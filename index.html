About Me:

I am currently a METEOR Postdoctoral Fellow at MIT's Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. I received my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Cornell University in August of 2023. I was advised by Professor Hadas Kress-Gazit and was part of the Verifiable Robotics Research Group

Research:

I work on human robot interaction and planning with minimal information. I am branching out into more areas of HRI (for example teams with multiple robots and people)! Shoot me a message if you are interested in collaborating.
